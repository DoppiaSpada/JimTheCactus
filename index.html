My video games

Jim The Cactus

This platform game is about a cactus trying to fight against worm-like enemies and to reach new worlds from one gate to the next. Help Jim to overcome his journey and collect gems as much as possible! Once your player gets enough green gems, unlock new superpowers and have fun with Jim!

Dona Game 2

"Dona Game 2" is the second version of the R. Donatelli high school video game. It's an educational project realized to show the everyday life inside the institute to new pupils. This is the cool and revolutionary enviroment where you can explore odd places and virtual classrooms either listening to intriguing teachers' explanations or make a try at solving physic or latin questions and problems.
