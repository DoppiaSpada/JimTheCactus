My video games

Jim The Cactus

This platformer game is about a cactus trying to fight kindred worm enemies and to reach new worlds from a gate to the next one. Help Jim to explore levels and collect gems as much as possible! Once your player get enough green gems, unlock new super powers and have fun with Jim!

Dona Game 2

"Dona Game 2" is the second version of R. Donatelli high school's videogame. It's an educational project realized to show to new pupils the everyday life inside R. Donatelli school in my town Terni. In this reality, you can explore diverse places and classrooms listening to teachers' explanations or attempting to figure out physic or latin questions and problems.
